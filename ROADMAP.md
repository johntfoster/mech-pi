# Roadmap

## v0.1 morning MVP

- Pi package with extension, skill, and prompt resources.
- Paper ingestion into `.mechpi/paper-map.json`.
- Equation focus by label or contents.
- `latexmk` compile loop.
- PDF preview hook.
- Symbol search.
- Lightweight LaTeX/reference/citation/index checks.
- Mechanics interrogation workflow.

## v0.2 notation intelligence

- Persistent `notation.yaml` registry.
- Macro expansion awareness from `def.tex`.
- Undefined/overloaded symbol reports.
- Scalar/vector/tensor typography checks.
- Equation-to-notation backlinks.

## v0.3 mechanics validators

- Phase-index consistency checks.
- Interaction/source-term sum rules.
- Balance-law templates for mass, momentum, energy, entropy.
- Dimensional database and equation dimension checks.
- Entropy inequality checklist and sign-convention tracker.

## v0.4 SymPy sandbox

- `theory/` workspace generator.
- Free-energy derivative and chemical-potential tools.
- Product-rule and Legendre-transform checks.
- Lightweight tensor/index object model with LaTeX export.

## v0.5 TUI research cockpit

- Equation browser.
- Paper map tree.
- Focus panel with source, rendered-ish equation, symbols, refs, and warnings.
- Question/interrogation wizard.
- Compile/watch status widget.
